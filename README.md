# Built For Small Business

Free all-in-one business management software for UK and Nigerian small businesses.

**Invoicing · Expenses · Payroll · Client Management · P&L Reports**

[builtforsmallbusiness.com](https://builtforsmallbusiness.com)

---

## What We Build

A free platform that gives small business owners the tools they actually need, without the enterprise price tag.

- 🧾 **Invoicing** — Create, send, and track invoices with PDF export
- 💸 **Expenses** — Log, categorise, and export expenses with VAT tracking
- 👥 **Payroll** — Run payroll with statutory compliance for the UK and Nigeria
- 📊 **P&L Reports** — Profit & loss reporting with PDF export
- 🏢 **Client Management** — Keep your clients and contacts organised
